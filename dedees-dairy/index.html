A Niagara Falls
Tradition

De Dee's Dairy is the kind of ice cream shop people drive across state lines for. Nestled on Niagara Falls Boulevard, we've been scooping up smiles for generations with our legendary portions, unbeatable prices, and the warmest welcome in town.

We proudly serve Perry's Ice Cream — New York's finest — with over 50 hand-dipped flavors, velvety soft serve, towering banana splits, decadent sundaes, thick milkshakes, and our famous ice cream flights. Every scoop is served fresh, every portion is ridiculous, and every visit feels like coming home.
