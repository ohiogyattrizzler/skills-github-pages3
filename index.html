Our Story

A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

Catalina's Bakery & Cafe is a family-owned gem on the banks of Chippawa Creek in Niagara Falls, Ontario. What started as a dream to share homemade Korean and Western baked goods has grown into a beloved neighborhood spot.

Every pastry is baked fresh with natural ingredients — no artificial sugars, no shortcuts. Just honest, delicious food and drinks made with care, served alongside a beautiful river view and the warmest hospitality you'll find.
